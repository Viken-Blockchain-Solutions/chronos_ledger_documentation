    Chronos Ledger Early Adopter NFT Program - Crafted for Visionaries

    The Chronos Ledger Early Adopter NFT Program is more than a rewards initiative—it's an invitation to be part of a visionary community that values precision in time, finance, and innovation. Our first users will gain exclusive NFTs that carry not only long-term benefits but also profound symbolism tied to time cycles and mastery over financial systems.

    Why the Number 72?

    We have carefully chosen to issue 72 unique NFTs, a number that resonates deeply with the values of time and balance that Chronos Ledger embodies. This number reflects the precision of time cycles: - 72 years marks the Earth’s movement of one degree through the zodiac—an ancient symbol of cosmic order and progress. - In numerology, the number 72 symbolizes balance, power, and creation, all qualities central to the mission of Chronos Ledger: to help users balance time, projects, and financial management.

    These NFTs not only reward early adopters but also symbolize the eternal cycles of productivity and financial mastery. By owning one, you are committing to the mastery of time and efficiency in both business and personal growth.

    3 NFT Tiers, Tailored to Your Commitment

    The Early Adopter NFTs are divided into three exclusive tiers, each representing a different level of commitment. Whether you pay monthly, semi-annually, or annually, there’s an NFT tier that offers increasing benefits over time.

    • Timelocked for 12 months: Unlocks after maintaining 12 consecutive monthly payments.
    • Symbolism of 42: Represents balance and the pursuit of life’s meaning, echoing Chronos Ledger’s goal of balancing time and work.
    • Benefits:
    • Start with a 10% discount on your subscription.
    • 50% discount by the third year.
    • After three years, enjoy a 30% lifetime discount.

    Tier 2: Semi-Annual Subscription (18 NFTs)

    • Timelocked for 6 months: Unlocks after two consecutive 6-month payments.
    • Symbolism of 18: Represents prosperity and success—perfect for users building sustainable businesses.
    • Benefits:
    • Start with a 15% discount.
    • 50% discount by the second year.
    • After two years, receive a 40% lifetime discount.

    Tier 3: Annual Subscription (12 NFTs)

    • No timelock: Unlocks immediately upon committing to annual payments.
    • Symbolism of 12: Tied to cosmic cycles (12 months, 12 zodiac signs), representing completion and mastery.
    • Benefits:
    • Enjoy a 25% discount immediately.
    • Grow to 75% by the third year.
    • After three years, benefit from a 50% lifetime discount.

    Unlock Long-Term Benefits and Exclusive Rewards

    By participating in the Early Adopter NFT Program, you don’t just save on subscription fees. You gain exclusive, ongoing benefits that recognize your long-term loyalty and trust in Chronos Ledger’s vision.

    • AI Assistant Credits: Receive up to 50% off AI credits, making it easier to leverage our powerful AI tools for project estimation and time tracking.
