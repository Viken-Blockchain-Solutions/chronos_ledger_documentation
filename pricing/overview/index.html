    Freemium vs. Early Adopter vs. Premium Subscription Plans

    Freemium Plan (Designed for freelancers)

    • Price: Free
    • Features:
    • 2 active project (1 client, 1 Internal)
    • 1 client
    • 1 automatic invoice per month (supports both crypto and fiat)
    • Autimatic time tracking
    • Basic reporting: Weekly and monthly time tracking reports
    • 1 document upload per project
    • Standard support

    Early Adopter Plan (Limited-time plan with NFTs)

    • Price:
    • Monthly Subscription: $35/month
    • 6-Month Subscription: $180/6 months (equivalent to $30/month)

    • Annual Subscription: $300/year (equivalent to $25/month)

    • Features:

    • 3 active projects (depending on tier)
    • 3 clients
    • 2 users/team
    • Up to 3 automatic invoices per month (supports both crypto and fiat)
    • Automated time tracking
    • Advanced reporting: Weekly and monthly reports with more detailed filters
    • 3 document uploads per project
    • Priority support
    • Progressive discounts via the NFT program (starts at 10%-25% and increases over time)
    • Early access to beta features and participation as early testers

    Premium Plan (Designed for startups and enterprises)

    • Price:
    • Monthly Subscription: $49/month

    • Annual Subscription: $540/year ($45/month equivalent)

    • Features:

    • Unlimited projects
    • Unlimited clients
    • 8 users/team
    • Unlimited invoices (fully automated, both crypto and fiat)
    • Automated time tracking across all projects
    • Advanced reporting: Custom reports, filters, and detailed insights
    • Unlimited document uploads
    • Crypto payroll: Full-scale payroll processing in crypto and fiat
    • Priority support
    • Access to future features: AI-powered project estimation, advanced analytics, etc.
