    1. Introduction to Chronos Ledger

    Chronos Ledger is an all-in-one SaaS platform designed for time tracking, automated invoicing, and crypto payments. This solution is tailored for freelancers, startups, and enterprises, providing tools to automate time entries and invoice generation while integrating with crypto payment systems like Request Network.

    3. Freemium vs. Premium Versions

    Overview of Free and Paid Plans:

    Chronos Ledger offers two tiers: - Freemium: Suitable for freelancers managing basic time tracking and invoicing for up to 2 projects. - Premium: Designed for startups and enterprises, providing advanced tools like automated invoicing, crypto payments, and unlimited projects.

    Detailed Feature Comparison:

    Feature Freemium Premium
    Time Tracking 1 active project Unlimited projects and team members
    Invoicing 1 automatic invoice/month Unlimited, automated based on logged hours
    Crypto Payments Limited Full integration with Request Network
    Reporting Basic weekly reports Advanced reports with filters and customization
    Document Uploads 1 document per project Unlimited uploads
    Client Management Up to 2 clients Unlimited clients
    Support Standard Premium support with priority access

    4. Technical Architecture

    System Overview:

    Chronos Ledger is built using Next.js (frontend) and Node.js (backend), with PostgreSQL for data storage and Request Network for handling crypto payments.

    Include a system architecture diagram here.
    Description: Visual showing the interaction between the frontend (Next.js), backend (Node.js), and third-party services like Request Network for crypto payments and PostgreSQL for data storage.

    Security and Compliance:

    Data is protected through SSL encryption, role-based access control (RBAC), and AML/KYC compliance for crypto payments, ensuring secure and private transactions.

    5. User Roles and Permissions

    CEO/Owner Access:

    Full system access to manage financials, projects, and reporting tools.

    CFO/Finance Access:

    Access to financial operations such as payroll, expenses, and invoices.

    Employee Access:

    Limited access to time tracking and assigned projects.

    Coming Soon: Ability to create custom roles with specific access levels.

    6. Automated Time Tracking

    Chronos Ledger allows users to configure automated time tracking through predefined work hours for projects. A cron job handles the automation of time logging, ensuring that hours are captured based on the user’s input.

    7. Automated Invoicing and Payments

    Invoices are automatically generated based on the time logged per project. Users can customize invoices and choose to send them in either crypto or fiat, with the ability to track invoice statuses and notifications when payments are made.

    8. Expense and Payroll Management

    Manage employee payroll with options for crypto payments. Employees can submit expenses for approval, and payroll is processed based on the hours logged, with crypto-to-fiat conversion available for payroll if required.

    9. Reporting and Analytics

    Chronos Ledger provides detailed reports based on time tracking, project performance, and invoicing data. Reports are customizable, allowing users to generate insights based on specific client needs, time frames, or project metrics.

    14. FAQs and Troubleshooting

    • How do I unlock my Early Adopter NFT?

    • For monthly subscribers, your NFT unlocks after 12 consecutive months of payments. For 6-month subscribers, your NFT unlocks after two consecutive semi-annual payments. For annual subscribers, your NFT is available immediately after the first payment.

    • Can I trade my NFT?

    • Yes! Once your NFT is unlocked, you can trade or sell it on secondary markets, giving it future value based on the embedded benefits.

    • What happens if I stop my subscription before my NFT unlocks?

    • If your subscription is canceled before the timelock is over, your NFT will remain locked, and you will not be able to trade or sell it.
